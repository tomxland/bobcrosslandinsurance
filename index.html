About Bob

Bob Crossland has lived and worked in Northern Virginia since 1980. For more than 20 years, he’s specialized in selling insurance and financial products. His resume includes working for major insurance companies and agencies, running his own property & casualty agency, and working for a well-known Wall Street brokerage firm. Now totally independent, he founded Crossland Insurance to provide benefit solutions – health, life, disability, dental and long term care insurance – to companies and individuals & families, placing an emphasis on containing costs while enhancing value. Bob has also been certified for several years by AHIP (America’s Health Insurance Plans, our trade association) to market Medicare supplemental products, Medicare Advantage, and Part D Plans to the senior markets and is often asked to speak publicly about Medicare and the Affordable Care Act. He often partners with other local insurance agencies and financial advisors as a resource to provide expertise, advice and solutions for benefit needs. With a reputation as a problem solver, Bob states, “I help small companies - and individuals - have big company benefits.”
